About Me Entry

Hello, my name is John Padron, better known as "Remer". I was born and raised in San Antonio Texas, and there has not been a moment in my life I didn't have a hand in art. The information I have here won’t be something that should be taken as facts or anything concreate, more of a journal with tips and notes logged from doing murals myself, and in time I hope to invite guest artists for journal entries.

Early on, graffiti started it all for me. And here i am still learning and improving, while painting murals. When i was younger, seeking guidance wasn’t something i knew where to begin, it wasn’t till' years later that I felt I started to earn opportunities. It’s worth mentioning that there is still a world of things I don’t know about murals, but there are also a handful of things I’ve learned that may or may not be something that can help the next person, so I hope to create a space that encourages that drive to tackle new things in art, read about different art topics and keep up with me on my art ventures.

As an artist all depending on your resources available you may or may not have access to everything you need to help you grow, or you may be missing guidance in a way you feel helpless in finding. The goal here is to start sharing experiences and lessons learned in mural painting and everything leading up to putting images on walls and other mediums. starting with experiences I’ve had, hopefully inviting and lifting other voices willing to chime in and lend a though in helping. With this effort I hope to create a space where anyone seeking information or advice can feel welcomed and guided.
